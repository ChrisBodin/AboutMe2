Rock Climbing

Rock Climbing, specifically Bouldering is a hobby I enjoy because of the strength and puzzle aspects. It is a sport in which you have a wall around 3-5 meters tall that you climb up to reach the top whether that is a top hold or the top of the wall. It is very fun because you need to figure out how to climb the route and then be strong enough, technically, physically, and mentally. It is very enjoyable and you can do it outside on rock faces or indoors in indoor gyms.
